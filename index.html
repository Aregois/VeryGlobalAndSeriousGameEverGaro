Controls & tips

Movement & combat

  • WASD to move, mouse to aim, left click to fire.
  • 1-8 (or scroll/Q/E) to switch weapons; 8 is the Knife.
  • ESC or P to pause/unlock the mouse; starting/resuming captures the pointer.
  • Press R from the start/pause screens for a quick restart; the HUD clock tracks your run time.
  • Explosions hurt you—give rockets and kamikazes space.

Pickups & buffs

  • Grab health, armor, and ammo (shells/SMG/rockets/cells/cannonballs) between waves.
  • Purple Serious Damage doubles outgoing damage; teal Speed boosts movement.
  • Buff bars show remaining timers; the boss bar appears when a boss-tier enemy is active.

Settings

  • Audio/Music toggles + sliders balance SFX and soundtrack levels.
  • Effects toggle removes shake/flash; sensitivity and crosshair size/color tune aim feel.
  • Stats toggle shows FPS/frame time; Reset settings restores defaults.
